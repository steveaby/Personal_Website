Vector Quest: A Faster and Better Open-Domain Question-Answering System

Open-domain Question Answering (QA) systems are designed to answer user questions using unrestricted knowledge. Unlike traditional systems that require users to provide a paragraph for context (similar to reading comprehension), Vector Quest enables users to ask questions without providing source material.

We proposed a unique methodology for storing the model's knowledge, allowing us to predict answers 50 times faster than existing systems. Our approach was compared against models working on SQuAD datasets and proved to be a superior alternative in terms of both accuracy and time complexity for open-domain scenarios.
